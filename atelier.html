Every seal follows the classical reading order — top-right, bottom-right, top-left, bottom-left (千→觅→量→子) — with carved-stone edges simulated in code, and the final marks are pure vector paths: no fonts, no images, sharp at any size. 所有印章均遵循传统回文读序(右上→右下→左上→左下:千→觅→量→子),石刻毛边由代码模拟;定稿印章为纯矢量路径 —— 不依赖字体与图片,任何尺寸都锐利。
