01 · The pulse01 · 搏动

A muscle that
keeps time.一块
守着时间的肌肉。

Sixty times a minute, in the dark, it tightens and lets go — a rhythm older than thought.每分钟六十次,在黑暗里收紧、松开 —— 一种比思想更古老的节律。

02 · The tide02 · 潮汐

Each beat moves
a tide of blood.每一次搏动
推动一场血的潮汐。

A cupful, forward, through chambers it will never see — carried by pressure, guided by two thin edges.一杯血,向前,穿过它永不得见的心腔 —— 由压力推送,由两片薄缘引导。

03 · The meeting03 · 对合

Two edges
must meet.两片薄缘,
必须相遇。

The mitral valve — two leaflets that close, twice a second, in perfect apposition. When they miss, the tide turns back.二尖瓣 —— 两片瓣叶,每秒两次严丝合缝地闭合。一旦错开,潮水便倒流。

04 · The reading04 · 读懂

We taught a machine
to feel for it.我们教会机器
去感知那一刻。

AutoClip traces the two edges in real time — finding the meeting a surgeon feels but cannot see.AutoClip 实时描摹这两片缘 —— 找到那个外科医生能感知、却无法亲眼看见的相遇。

The study behind the poem诗背后的研究

Not an illustration — research, running now across three countries.这不是插画 —— 而是一项正在进行的研究,横跨三国。

AutoClip develops real-time AI to trace the mitral leaflets during transcatheter repair. I'm its sponsor-investigator and PI — a structural heart interventionist who both performs these procedures and builds the models that may one day guide them.AutoClip 开发用于经导管修复中实时描摹二尖瓣瓣叶的 AI。我是它的发起人与主要研究者 —— 一名既亲手做这些手术、也亲手构建引导模型的结构性心脏病介入医生。
